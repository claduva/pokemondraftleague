About

This website is a hobby I have used to learn web development. It is designed to host draft leagues for Pokemon, automating most of the functions of managing a draft league. Site artwork credit to Amir Alexander unless otherwise specified.

Support Me

This website is and always will be free to use. However, it does cost me about $20 a month for hosting costs. So if you like the site and would like to support my work, you can buy me a coffee!
